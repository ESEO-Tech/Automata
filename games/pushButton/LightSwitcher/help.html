Turn the light on or off when the button state changes. Two scenarios can be considered:

  • Turn the light on when the button is pressed; turn the light off when the button is released.
  • Toggle the light when the button transitions from the released state to the pressed state.

Inputs

B
(Button) 		1 The button is pressed
0 The button is released

Outputs

L
(Light) 		1 Turn on the light
0 Turn off the light
