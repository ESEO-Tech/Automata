Program the robot to find the orange circle, behind the wall.

Tip: You can program the same action several times with different conditions, e.g. 'Go ahead until finding a wall in front.' or 'Go ahead while there is a wall on the right.'

Sensors

WL
(Wall on the Left) 		1 There is a wall on the left side of the robot
0 There is no obstacle on the left side of the robot
WR
(Wall on the Right) 		1 There is a wall on the right side of the robot
0 There is no obstacle on the right side of the robot
WA
(Wall Ahead) 		1 There is a wall in front of the robot
0 There is no obstacle in front of the robot
EOR
(End Of Rotation) 		1 The robot is oriented along the North-South or East-West axis
0 The robot has an intermediate orientation

Actuators

LF
(Left wheel Forward) 		1 The left wheel turns in the forward direction
0 No effect
LB
(Left wheel Backward) 		1 The left wheel turns in the backward direction
0 No effect
RF
(Right wheel Forward) 		1 The right wheel turns in the forward direction
0 No effect
RB
(Right wheel Backward) 		1 The right wheel turns in the backward direction
0 No effect

Setting both Forward and Backward at the same time for the same wheel has no effect.
